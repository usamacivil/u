Professional Summary

Assistant Professor of Civil Engineering with specialized expertise in Geotechnical Earthquake Engineering and Soil-Structure Interaction (SSI)[cite: 2, 6]. My research bridges the gap between geotechnical and structural disciplines, utilizing high-fidelity numerical modeling and experimental investigations to evaluate the dynamic response of soil-pile-structure systems[cite: 7]. I am an expert in nonlinear soil behavior, foundation dynamics, and the seismic performance of deep foundations under multi-directional loading[cite: 8].

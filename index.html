Professional Summary

Assistant Professor of Civil Engineering with specialized expertise in Geotechnical Earthquake Engineering and Soil-Structure Interaction (SSI). My research bridges the gap between geotechnical and structural disciplines, utilizing high-fidelity numerical modeling and experimental investigations to evaluate the dynamic response of soil-pile-structure systems. I am an expert in nonlinear soil behavior, foundation dynamics, and the seismic performance of deep foundations under multi-directional loading. I am actively engaged in funded research initiatives and graduate supervision, with specific experience mentoring diverse cohorts within the International Graduate Program.

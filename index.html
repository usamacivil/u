Area of Focus

Research Interests

My work addresses fundamental challenges in geotechnical earthquake engineering, combining rigorous numerical methods with large-scale experimental validation.

Earthquake Engineering

Seismic hazard assessment, ground motion characterization, and the performance-based design of civil infrastructure under rare and extreme earthquake events.

Key Topics

Site response · Liquefaction · Seismic risk

Soil–Structure Interaction

Nonlinear SSI analysis for bridges, buildings, and offshore structures, with emphasis on kinematic and inertial coupling mechanisms during seismic loading.

Key Topics

Foundation impedance · DSSI · Nonlinear springs

Dynamic Response of Piles

Experimental and computational investigation of single and group pile response, including 1g shake table campaigns and large-scale centrifuge testing programs.

Key Topics

Kinematic forces · p-y curves · Pile-group effects
